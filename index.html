Birthday Party Terms

RSVP
Répondez s'il vous plaît, a French phrase meaning "please respond"used to confirm attendance.
Party Favour
A small gift given to guests as a thank you for attending the party
Pinata
A decorated container filled with candy and toys that is broken open as a part of celebration
